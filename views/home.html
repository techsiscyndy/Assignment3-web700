Introduction to HTML5

This site utilizes HTML5: a powerful and versatile markup language used for structuring and presenting content on the World Wide Web. HTML5 introduces numerous features and improvements over its predecessors, including enhanced support for multimedia elements such as audio and video, more robust APIs for complex web applications, and better semantic elements for improved accessibility and SEO. As the backbone of modern web development, HTML5 ensures a rich and dynamic user experience across diverse devices and platforms.
